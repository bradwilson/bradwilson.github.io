Best of Music

Every year, I list some of the music that I found that I felt represented the best of the respective genres. It isn't my job to review music, so this "best of" list is a personal endeavor, not an exhaustive attempt to locate the very best music of the year.

Below you'll find the list of the years and the albums I selected; each year's page contains brief descriptions of the albums as well as embedded YouTube videos, so they can be quite slow to load.
